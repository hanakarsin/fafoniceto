Former Jenifa's Diary Actress Accuses Pastor Timilehin Of Sexually Abusing Her Sister (Video)

Former actress in the popular Nigerian series, Jenifa’s Diary, Julianna Olayode, also known as Toyosi, has accused Pastor Timilehin Adigun of sexually abusing her sister.

The actress in a video shared on her Instagram account on Wednesday reacted to the statement of the cleric that she came to his office and she was shaking because she knew she had wronged him.

According to Julianna, she did not shake as claimed by the pastor, however, she got to know that he allegedly invited her sister over from school, lodged her in a hotel, and was touching her inappropriately.

The actress also accused Pastor Timilehin of asking her sister to allow him sleep with her.

She noted that this made her anger and shake when she went to see him in his church.

Julianna added that she suspected a while back that the cleric was abusing her sister sexually and she had to visit him to warn him never to touch her sister.

She noted that pastor Timilehin denied the allegation, accusing her of using his ”weakness and struggles” against him.
