Turning a year older comes with wisdom, knowledge and for some people like the gorgeous Swahili TV news anchor Mwanahamisi Hamadi, new opportunities for growth.

She turned a year older and celebrated her new year on her Facebook page while also letting her fans know about her new radio show.

She wrote:

''It feels great to be Older. Wisdom, knowledge and new outlook increasing. I thank God for the new year and the new assignments for my career. Let's meet every Friday at 8 @Radiocitizen. Happy Birthday to me. I love you all.''
