Karma - Age, Family, Bio

June 20, 2024
Post

Multi-talented personality and musician also known as Karma Kalypso, who became popular for her online beauty and dance tutorials. She gained fame through her Instagram account and her YouTube channels Clarissa May, Live on Pointe, and Life With Clarissa May. Also a classically trained composer and electronic musician, she is known for her original compositions and live electronic performances. 

Before Fame

She began performing at the early age of 13, playing acoustic gigs around Southern California. She went on to graduate with a BA in Sound Design and Music Production.

Trivia

She's popular on Instagram @karma, as well as her Karma Official YouTube channel.  She began writing music for piano at the age of 13, and at that time she was also a ballet dancer training 30+ hours a week.

Family Life

She has lived with her younger sister Audrey. She has a Pomsky puppy named Mochi, and a black cat named Toothless. 

Associated With

Like fellow beauty YouTuber Zoe Sugg, she is a partner of StyleHaul.
