Meet beautiful lawyer's wife hawking meals using wheelbarrow

July 04, 2024
Post

- A woman who is a graduate has caused a stir online for using a wheelbarrow to sell various items

- The woman, reportedly the wife of a lawyer, took the decision because she was unemployed and needed a source of income

- She has however given details about how her profit soared up to about 300% of her capital a few months after starting the business

Photos of a beautiful woman who has graduated from the university but has opted to sell food items on a wheelbarrow have gone viral on the internet.

The unnamed woman, who is reportedly a mother and the wife of a lawyer, took the bold decision as securing a job became a challenge after school.

Taking to her Instagram account to share what she does, she explained that she believes God takes people out of their comfort zone if he wants to bless them.

She claimed she was not ashamed of her newfound job and has been at the receiving end of praises and criticisms in equal measures ever since she started the business.

"I sell food on a wheelbarrow, yes I went to school, a mother, married to a lawyer, but some will ask why am I doing this? Some even wondered why I selling food in a wheelbarrow yet am to beautiful," narrated the lady.

The woman went on to say she felt ashamed when she first took the wheelbarrow out as she wondered what her family and friends would say.

She however encouraged herself and said that family and friends would desert her if she stays hungry.

I laughed, see when God want to bless you he takes you out of your comfort zone, am not ashamed of my hustle, I got praises and criticised for my hustle. The first time I took my wheelbarrow I almost went back, I was ashamed at first," she said.

On the first day, she added, she had sold all the food by 1pm and made about 60% profit and in two months, her profit had shot up to 300% of her initial investment.

The lady concluded her story by adding that she is certain God would bless the efforts of those who take bold decisions in life, as He is the one who gives ideas about how to create wealth.

"So dear friends I have seen a lady that fried akara to build a house and send her children to the university, that small business that u think you can't do because you are too big for it maybe your stepping stone to greatness, " she advised other women
