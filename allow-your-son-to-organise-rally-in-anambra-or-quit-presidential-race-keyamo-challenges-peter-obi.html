Allow Your Son To Organise Rally In Anambra Or Quit Presidential Race, Keyamo Challenges Peter Obi

April 12, 2024
Post

The Spokesperson for the All Progressives Congress (APC) Presidential Campaign Council, Festus Keyamo, has challenged the Presidential candidate of the Labour Party (LP), Peter Obi, on organizing a well-coordinated campaign rally.

Naija News reports that youths in the ruling party on Saturday held a solidarity rally for the presidential candidate of the APC, Bola Ahmed Tinubu.

The rally, tagged ‘Eko Youth Walk’ and organised by the son of the APC presidential candidate, Seyi Tinubu, was held at Police College, Ikeja GRA.

Following the development, Keyamo took to his verified Twitter handle on Saturday to describe the rally as well-organized.

The minister, however, challenged Obi to allow his son to hold a similar walk in Awka, Anambra State capital within one week, especially on a Monday or dump his presidential ambition.

He wrote: “Lagos Walk organised by ⁦@STinubu⁩ in a well-organised Lagos State. This is an open challenge to Peter Obi to allow his son to hold a similar walk in Awka, Anambra State, where he governed for 8 years within the next one week, especially on a Monday, or throw in the towel!”
