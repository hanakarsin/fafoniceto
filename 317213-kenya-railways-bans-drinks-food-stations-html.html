Kenya Railways bans drinks, food from outside in its stations

June 23, 2024
Post

- The move has angered many Kenyans who termed the directive ill-timed

- A section of citizens have asked activist Okiya Omtatah to come to their rescue

- Other Kenyans claimed the state corporation is "colonising" them through the Chinese who operate the trains

The Kenya Railways has banned drinks and food in its termini and stations.

The state corporation said anyone found with food or any kind of drinks will have it confiscated without any chance of repossession.
