  • The news, however, left fans with mixed reactions as some preferred a different candidate while others thought her academic excellence will serve the gaming show

The Big Bang Theory actress Mayim Bialik has made history as the first female to be given a permanent host job on Jeopardy! Show.

Bialik gets permanent TV show hosting gig

On Wednesday, Bialik and Jeopardy! Executive producer Mike Richards were named the two new hosts of the long-running game show.

The 46-year-old executive producer will be hosting the daily syndicated version of the popular show, while the 45-year-old Bialik will host prime-time and spinoff versions of Jeopardy!.
