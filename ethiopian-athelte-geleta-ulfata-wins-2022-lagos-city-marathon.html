Ethiopian Athelte, Geleta Ulfata Wins 2022 Lagos City Marathon

June 17, 2024
Post

Ethiopian athlete, Geleta Ulfata on Saturday won the 7th edition of the Access Bank Lagos City Marathon.

Naija News reports that Ulfata beats David Barmasai of Kenya who clocked 2.13.27 to take the second medal and Naibe Emmanuel, also of Kenya with a time of 2.14.42 finishing 3rd on the log.

Ulfata, who clocked 2.11.58, won the grand prize of $30,000.
