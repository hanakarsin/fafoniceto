TikTok star who is best recognized for his tooturnttony account where he posts comedy videos, often involving his family and his ducks. His videos have earned him over 16 million followers on the platform. In early 2021, he started a YouTube channel called TooTurntTony where his first video received over 50,000 views in its first few days. He has been known for his refreshing disregard for social norms and his open promotion of excess. 

Before Fame

He worked at a production company in New York City, and began posting videos during the COVID-19 pandemic stay-at-home order. He posted his first TikTok video in January 2020.

Trivia

He had ducks for several years before he began posting on TikTok, due to the fact that he was allergic to normal pets. His first TikTok video to receive over 1 million views is a comedy skit he filmed with his duck. He began working with the WME talent agency in December 2020.               

Family Life

His siblings Dominic and Maria have appeared on his tooturnttony Instagram account. His sister works as his assistant. His mom and Nonnie frequently appear in his videos. He has two Pekin ducks. He credits his family dynamic as a big reason he gained fame. 

Associated With

He posted a TikTok video set to a Kodak Black song in May 2020.
