Nyota Ndogo is one of the most respected musicians in Kenya who has thrived for over two decades.

For her to remain at the top, the star has always evaded controversies and scandals and that has not only earned her massive respect but also a huge fanbase.

Most people who follow or love her music love her for her art and more; her personality.

The musician recently proved yet again that she is one humble creature with her response to a hater who wanted to drag her down.

The star did not caption the photo but one critic wanted to flex with her but was embarrassed terribly by Nyota's comeback.

The critic who goes by Kajala Harmo1 shamelessly told Nyota that she needed to change her sofa set owing to her celebrity status.

''Change your sofas mamaaaa,''

Well, being a mature artiste with many years of experience in the game, Nyota did not go ham on her attacker but chose humility instead.

In a calm and her real demeanour, Nyota just told the critic that times are hard hence she cannot just splash money on a makeover just because of the attacker's opinion.
