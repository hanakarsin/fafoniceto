Jonathan Is Squeezing The Life Out Of PDP Kwankwaso

February 18, 2024
Post

Kano State Governor, Rabiu Musa Kwankwaso, has denied ever describing President Goodluck Jonathan as evil due to the hat he wears.

The Governor was speaking in a recent two hour media chat and said: “There was never a time I referred to Mr President or anybody wearing a hat as evil. What I actually said was that President Jonathan has tightened the umbrella until he made a hat that only fits him and few others.”

The Governor explained that the tightened hat represents PDP, because it was created in 1998 and had accepted different politicians from all parts of the country and different points of views till GEJ squeezed it into a hat for himself.

He continued: “My good friends Rotimi Amaechi and Peter Odili as well as other Nigerians from the South-South wear hats and are also good people.”

He also denied ever calling Secretary to the Federation, Anyim Pius Anyim and Vice President, Namadi Sambo Vultures and revealed they where his good friends.

He said: “The vultures I was referring to are the three governors who were present at the President’s Kano visit and Godswill Akpabio is one of them.”

He wants to be anointed presidential candidate if Jonathan fails to get the ticket, while between the remaining two, one would be made the vice president and other, secretary to the federation.”
