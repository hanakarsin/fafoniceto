How Boko Haram Killed 8,000 Members Of My Church - Pastor

June 18, 2024
Post

Reverend Samuel Dali, the president of Ekklisiyar Yan'uwa A Nigeria, EYN (Church of the Brethren in Nigeria), has revealed how Boko Haram killed about 8,000 of his members.

Expressing hopes that the end of the terror group is near, the cleric said some of the church facilities in Adamawa, Yobe and Borno states have been destroyed in different Boko haram attacks.

[article_adwert]

Speaking with journalists in his Jos office recently, Reverend Dali lamented that aside from the 70% of his church members that have been killed,  most of his pastors have lost their jobs as their places of worship has been burnt and members killed.
