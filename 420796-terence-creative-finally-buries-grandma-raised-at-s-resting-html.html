Comedian Terence Creative has finally bid farewell to his beloved grandmother, Grace Wambui, who passed away more than a week ago after a long illness.

Through his Instagram page, the funny man shared photos and videos of the burial ceremony showing some of the events that took place on the final day, on Saturday, July 31.

From footage shared on his Instagram Stories, an emotional Terence could be seen in tears as he viewed the body of his late grandmother at the mortuary, whom he credits for making him the man he is today.

The content creator's family and friends held a short ceremony before leaving for the burial at an undisclosed location.

At the burial site, Terence gave an emotional story about the struggles his grandma went through in her final days and revealed she was battling diabetes.

He revealed they had rented a house for her while awaiting the one they were constructing for her to be completed, but she died before relishing it.

Terence said he got a message notifying him of her death, but at first, he thought it was a mistake until he was called and informed that she was gone.

He said she died a very peaceful death and noted he would miss her and hoped to meet her again in paradise.

Terence mourns grandma

As earlier reported by TUKO.co.ke, Terence paid tribute to his grandma in a heartfelt message posted on social media on Friday, July 23, saying he had plans in store for her.

He expressed gratitude to God for the times they shared, saying he cannot believe that he would not see her again.

“I thank God for the time He gave us with my grandma. Today I took the last selfie and hugged her for the last time. I had so many plans for her, but God had better plans,” he wrote.

Terence said her late grandmother was funnier than him, and they used to gossip together.

According to Terence, she played a crucial role in his life and that of his brothers. He also expressed gratitude to God for allowing her to see his children.
