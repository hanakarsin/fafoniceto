Cee-C Recounts How Torn Bikini Left Her Embarrassed At Zanzibar

July 09, 2024
Post

Big Brother Naija ‘All-Stars’ housemate, Cee-C has recounted how a torn bikini left her embarrassed during a vacation in Zanzibar.

Naija News reports that the reality TV star recalled the wardrobe malfunction experience while introducing herself to other housemates last night.

Cee-C said she is a lawyer, a brand influencer like her colleagues and also into movie productions.

Speaking on the most embarrassing moment in her life, Cee-C disclosed that while she was under the water during a vacation, a part of her body she wanted to be covered was exposed, and she didn’t notice until someone pointed it out.

Why I Turned Down Offer To Participate In BBNaija All-Stars

Meanwhile, former Big Brother Naija 2019 Housemate, Tacha has given her reason for not participating in the ongoing ‘All-Stars’ reality show.

Naija News reports that selected housemates from previous editions of the show, like Mercy, WhiteMoney, Venita, Neo, Ike, Cee-C, Cross, Angel and Alex, Seyi, Kiddwaya, among others, returned to the reality TV show on Sunday.

Speaking about her absence after former colleague, Mike Edwards replied media personality, Toke Makinwa’s question on housemates that should have been added but wasn’t, the social media influencer said that the organizers of the show had refused to pay her to come back.

According to the Rivers-born BBN housemate, they refused to pay after her price went up.
