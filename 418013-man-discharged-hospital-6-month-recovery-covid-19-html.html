Man Discharged from Hospital after 6-Month Recovery from COVID-19

April 05, 2024
Post
  • A 54-year-old man is finally being discharged from hospital after a six month long battle with COVID-19
  • The incredible patient had spent most of his time in ICU recovering from the devastating disease
  • Social media users took to the comments section and wished the local man well as he continued to recover

A local man has certainly overcome the odds, spending a record six months in recovery after he contracted the coronavirus disease.

After 166 days in the hospital, 164 of which were spent in ICU, Orient Heights resident, Ally Khan, finally went home on Wednesday.

The 54-year-old has no idea where he contracted the virus but is thought to have acquired the infection sometime around January 12. This despite the fact that he and his wife, Rahouda, took extra careful health and safety protocols.
