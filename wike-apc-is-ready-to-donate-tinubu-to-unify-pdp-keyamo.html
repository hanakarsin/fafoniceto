APC Is Ready To Donate Tinubu To Unify PDP Keyamo

April 07, 2024
Post

The Minister of State for Labour and Employment, Festus Keyamo, has pitched in on the crisis rocking the Peoples Democratic Party, PDP.

Naija News reports the PDP have been in a turbulent state after it’s presidential primaries which produced Atiku Abubakar as it’s flag bearer.

The party erupted into crisis when Atiku opted for Governor Ifeanyi Okowa of Delta State as his running mate.

Rivers state governor, Nyesom Wike, who had claimed that the primaries were manipulated to favor Atiku, called for the resignation of the party’s national chairman, Iyorchia Ayu, for peace to be returned to the party.

However, Ayu have said that he has no intentions of stepping down till his tenure is over.

Speaking on the crisis in the PDP via a series of Tweet, Keyamo said the All Progressives Congress, APC, is willing to donate its presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu, to help unify the PDP.

He wrote “If PDP begs us, we may consider donating the real UNIFIER, @officialABAT to assist in unifying their ranks to get them ready for their comprehensive defeat in 2023.

“We want a healthy opposition during the election. We don’t want the excuse that we fought with a disabled opponent.

“It is funny that other candidates spend all their energy to debunk all the achievements of @officialABAT, whereas they are not using that same energy to show their own achievements.”
