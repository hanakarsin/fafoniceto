Muthoni Wa Mukiri Expresses Desire to Get Pregnant: &quot;I'm Next&quot;

July 29, 2024
Post
  • Media personality Muthoni Wa Mukiri can't wait to see the fruits of her womb just a month since she held a ruracio
  • She shared a photo of heavily pregnant Venessa Mdee and mentioned how much she craves for the same state
  • Muthoni is looking forward to conceiving for her hubby Isaac, whom she said came to her life as a blessing

Former Inooro TV presenter Muthoni wa Mukiri has hopefully stated she wants to be a mother as soon as possible.

The media personality who recently tied the knot with his fiancé, Isaac, drew inspiration to get pregnant from Venessa Mdee after the latter announced her pregnancy on social media with beautiful photos.

Via her Instagram stories, Mukiri shared a photo of pregnant Venessa Mdee and her husband and she noted on the photo that she is the next one in line.

"I'm the next," she wrote.

Not so long ago, one month to be precise, Muthoni married her long-time lover, Isaac, in a colourful traditional ceremony, popularly known as ruracio.

This is the love of his life and he has disclosed how willing she is to make him a father.

Muthoni falls for man who didn't love her

As previously reported by TUKO.co.ke, Muthoni talked about loving someone who was not interested in her.

"When you love someone but they don't love you back, we automatically raise their value. There is this guy I was really into. We went on three dates and I started getting into him and it got to a point I would call and text him a lot," Muthoni recalled.

She added that the man went silent on her and she couldn't figure out her mistake:

"But then he stopped putting in efforts and I started thinking I was the problem. It was hard for me to understand why he decided to go silent on me. The first thing that automatically comes into your mind is maybe you did something wrong."
