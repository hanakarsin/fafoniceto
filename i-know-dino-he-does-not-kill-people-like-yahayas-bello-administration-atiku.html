I Know Dino, He Does Not Kill People Like Yahaya's Bello Administration

March 12, 2024
Post

Former Vice President, Atiku Abubakar has said that the Kogi State Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) candidate in the November 11 governorship election, Dino Melaye, would make one of the best governors in the country if voted for.

The former vice president stated this on Thursday during the party’s mega rally in Lokoja, Kogi State.

Atiku said that he has worked with Dino Melaye since 1999 and has found him as very reliable, diligent, intelligent and patriotic. He urged Kogi people to vote him in as their next governor.

Atiku argued that Melaye does not kill people like what the present administration led by Yahaya Bello is doing.

“Dino is committed, determined, courageous and well focused. He doesn’t kill people like what the present administration is doing in Kogi State. I am appealing to you to come out and vote for PDP and defend your vote,” Atiku stated.

Atiku, who berated Yahaya Bello for allegedly denying the PDP the use of Confluence Stadium Lokoja to conduct their campaign rally, said it was time to sack the ruling APC to put an end to unfavorable policies in Kogi State.

“They denied us the use of stadium in Osun State and we later used the party office for our rally. At the end, PDP sacked them from Government house. Now they denied us the use of the Confluence Stadium Lokoja and booked all the hotel just to stop us from coming here. Despite what they did, you all still came to the party Secretariat to show solidarity and support for Senator Dino Melaye,” he added.

According to him, the emergence of Dino Melaye as Governor will bring about good governance and development in Kogi State.
