Alex Ekubo And Fancy Acholonu's Secrets Leak In Private Chats (Details)

February 29, 2024
Post

Things are beginning to get messier between Nollywood actor, Alex Ekubo and his ex-fiance, Fancy Acholonu, as their private chats have emerged online.

Naija News reports that the former lovebirds have been making headlines following Fancy’s public apology about their failed wedding which she later claimed Alex forced her to put up online.

In the series of chats that emerged online, Fancy claimed everything seemed wrong in their relationship despite spending five years together.

A part of the chat alleged that Fancy was verbally and emotionally abused after their relationship was made public and they never got intimate.

In a conversation with Alex, Fancy analyzed how she wanted an intimate wedding but the actor was pushing to a lavish ceremony because of his colleagues.

The American model who seemed unable to contain the issues with Alex Ekubo told him she was calling off their wedding and he responded by saying the issue must not get to the internet.

Naija News recalls that on 26th August 2021, Fancy called off their wedding ceremony which was scheduled to hold in November.

While announcing their separation, a part of the social media post read: ‘Everyone should find their happiness and live in their truth.‘

However, in a statement shared on her Instagram page on Thursday night, December 29, 2022, Fancy apologized for hurting him and his family.

She also revealed how hard it has been for her since she lost him, adding that it felt like grief.

She further noted that her actions were out of character and realized that she could have controlled her emotions and not been so overreactive.

Alex Reacts

Responding to Fancy’s apology, Alex expressed appreciation to her and prayed that God will bless them all.

Hours after Alex’s reaction online, WhatApp chats between the actor and Fancy made the rounds online with many people speculating that the lovebirds have reconciled.

Fancy Speaks

However, while responding to a series of questions from her followers on Instagram, Fancy revealed that Alex compelled her to put up the apology post as a condition to reconcile and she fell for it.

She noted that irrespective of how things turned out between her and the actor, she is currently at peace and has a full understanding of everything.

According to Fancy, Alex is more concerned about his image and not the harm and pain he had also caused her.
