Gospel musician Ruth Matete has marked one year since the demise of her husband, Pastor John Apewajoye, better known as Beloved John.

In a lengthy post she shared on her Facebook page, Matete described the loss as one of the hardest moments in her life, terming her late husband as "irreplaceable".

By the time of John's death, the singer was two months pregnant. She pointed at God's grace for helping her pass through the difficult moment.

"It's been crazy. It's been hard. But God's grace has kept me. I was two months pregnant with my daughter when her father died. I didn't know how I was going to make it or if I was even going to make it. But God!" She said.

"My husband and I had plans, dreams, that we were going to work towards, but God had other plans," she added.

Ruth longed for the presence of her late husband, whom she believed would have been instrumental in the growth of her newborn daughter.

"I miss my husband so much. Every milestone my baby Toluwa achieves, I wish that her father would be here to celebrate it with me. But such is life. Some days are really hard. I have accepted the fact that healing will take time. I won't rush myself. I'll heal when I heal," she wrote.

Pastor John died out of a gas accident on April 11, 2020, just five months after the couple had tied the knot.

As reports indicated, the late John was fixing a faulty gas cylinder before the deadly accident.

The cylinder had been leaking the inflammable gas before igniting a fire.
