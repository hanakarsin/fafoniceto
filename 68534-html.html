Ekiti Election: Early Results Shows PDP Candidate, Ayo Fayose, Leading The Polls

February 09, 2024
Post

The early results from the Ekiti Governorship Elections that held today indicates that the flag bearer of the People’s Democratic Party, PDP, Ayodele Fayose, is already leading with a wide margin and he may likely win the final votes.

Youths celebrating PDP victory

The Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) ad hoc staffs have started collating the results in almost all the 16 local governments areas and they are now announcing the results of the elections at the various polling units and wards across the state.

Meanwhile after casting his votes, Fayose said he would accept the outcome of the election “whichever way it goes.”
