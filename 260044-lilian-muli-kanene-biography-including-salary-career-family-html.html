Lilian Muli education

She went to Loreto Convent, Msongari for her secondary school training. She continued to Daystar University where she got her bachelor's in communication and news-casting. She later joined UoN for a Masters in Strategic Management.

Work engagements and career progression

Lilian first worked at KTN creation division in 2005. After a couple of fizzled endeavours, she had her big break when she went to convey a few contents to the Standard Media's judges and left them dumbfounded with her capacity.

After 6 years, she cleared out from KTN and left for Citizen Television where she has been ever since her transition. She is known to host famous citizen television shows besides anchoring. One of the shows that were particularly popular was the Slim Possible Show which encouraged women to shed extra weight for a coveted price at the end of the challenge.

Lillian Muli spouse

Back then Lilian Muli's bae and significant other was Moses Njuguna Kanene. The two dated for a long time before Moses popped the question within KTN's vicinity. The news of their engagement was received with so much joy countrywide.

It was on Friday 28, 2008, soon after Muli had completed her early afternoon newscasting that Moses entered carrying a cake, an improved little box and a bunch of delightful blooms. Before every one of her associates, he went down on one knee and proposed to an elated Lilian.

She said yes and the two went on to plan a future together, starting with a beautiful and lavish wedding ceremony. After their marriage, Lilian became popularly known as Lilian Muli Kanene.

Lilian Muli divorce

Sadly, her marriage was not to last. After some time, speculations emerged that she was separated from her former husband. This ended up in a divorce with adultery, desertion, and cruelty cited as reasons for divorce.

However, she chose to keep things quiet for a while. Lilian Muli kids

The anchor gave birth to her first child, Joshua in 2010 after her marriage. She later gave birth to her second son, Liam Francis. The father of Lilian Muli's baby Liam is Jared Nevaton Ombongi, Shabana F.C boss.

The idea of Lilian Muli's daughter existence has been rumoured for a while now. However, the truth is that she is a mother of two boys. She recently wished a baby by the name Zuri a happy birthday as she turned one, and this may have been the trigger as many thought that she had a girl.
