- Man United vs LASK saw the homeside secure a scrappy win at Old Trafford in the Europa League second leg fixture

- United had to come from a goal down to sink a stubborn LASK in the second leg, with Anthony Martial getting the job done

- The Red Devils will take on Copenhagen in the quarters on Monday, August 10, a s they continue their impressive campaign in the competition

Man United have booked a place in the 2019/20 Europa League quarter-finals following their 7-1 aggregate win over LASK in the Round of 16.

United confirmed their place in the last eight of the competition after a 2-1 win against LASK in the second leg of the tie played at Old Trafford on Wednesday night, August 5.
