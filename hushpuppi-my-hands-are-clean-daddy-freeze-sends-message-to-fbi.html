Hushpuppi: My Hands Are Clean

May 19, 2024
Post

Popular OAP, Ifedayo Olarinde aka Daddy Freeze, has stated that he has nothing to hide over his relationship with the confessed fraudster, Ramon Abbas, alias Ray Hushpuppi.

Recall that Hushpuppi, an Instagram celebrity, was arrested last year in Dubai for engaging in fraudulent activities.

Following his arrest and recent confessing nailing DCP Abba Kyari, some Nigerians on social media referenced Daddy Freeze’s 2019 visit to Hushpuppi’s Palazzo Versace home in Dubai saying it was only a matter of time before the FBI came after the controversial broadcaster.

But reacting in an Instagram video, Daddy Freeze disclosed that he was ready to meet the FBI if he is indicted.

He said: “You guys need to get away from this mentality that anybody who gets to hang out with anybody must be collecting money from that person. I will not lie I am disappointed in Hush and if I see him I will tell him. Because you fed the rumours, tomorrow if somebody is buying a Ferrari then the person must be a thief

“Someone said Freeze dey fear, I don talk before when you no get sense you go dey talk nonsense, I don talk am say if FBI wants to question me, make them dare me I ready answer anybody question,” he said in pidgin.

“What do I have to hide? Do I have anything to hide? They will come and sit down and look at all my bank details. Let them just look…let them check all the notes I have and see if there is anything that looks like ‘Dubai money’ that can be traced to Hushpuppi, they should come, for free. It is when you have something to hide that you will be worried about. What I wanted from Hushpuppi was an interview.”
