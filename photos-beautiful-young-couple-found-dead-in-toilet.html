Beautiful Young Couple Found Dead In Toilet

February 25, 2024
Post

Akinro Blessing Ojay and his wife, Mary Igwe, have been found dead in their toilet at their home in Akure.

According to reports, the young couple were found dead on Tuesday, Nov 23, 2021.

The late couple were quite expressive with their love for each other before their sudden death on Tuesday.

Akinro was seen on WhatsApp at 11 pm on Sunday, Nov. 21 with his last Facebook post was on Nov. 13.

The couple were reported missing after their loved ones noticed their absence for days. A missing person report was filed at a police station which led the police officers to break into the house to find the couple locked in their toilet.

Their bodies showed signs that they had been murdered.

The bodies have been deposited in the morgue.

Akinro and Mary, who married at a young age, constantly displayed their love for each other on Facebook.

In one post, Akinro said convincing Mary to marry him is his “most brilliant achievement”.

In her last Facebook post, Mary referred to Akinro as her “joy giver” and “soul mate”.
