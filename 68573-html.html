Adokiye who became popular after her semi-nude photos leaked online has joined the likes of singer, 2face Idibia, Ice Prince, Nollywood actress, Joke Silva and Chika Ike amongst others who have called for the release of the Chibok school girls.

Meanwhile on Friday, the names and photographs of the 197 girls still being held by the Boko Haram terrorist sect appeared in ThisDay newspaper.
