One of the 5 co-founders of the enormously popular YouTube channel Dude Perfect, which has attracted over 57 million subscribers. The channel is a sports and entertainment brand that specializes in trick shots and comedy. 

Before Fame

He came together with Coby and Cory Cotton, Cody Jones, and Tyler Toney to form the YouTube channel in June of 2009 while attending Texas A&M University. 

Trivia

His nickname in the group is The Purple Hoser. He posted a photo with Zac Efron to his garretthilbert Instagram account and the photo has received over 61 thousand hearts. 

Family Life

He married a woman named Kristin in 2011. The two have sons named Owen and Lincoln and a daughter named Iris.  

Associated With

He and Dude Perfect have collaborated with athletes like Drew Brees and Serena Williams.
